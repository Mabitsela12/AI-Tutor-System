Welcome to the AI Tutor System

This user guide will help you navigate and make the most of your AI Tutor system. Learn how to log in, explore content for each grade level, use AI tools, and more.

1. Getting started

Login and sign up

- Go to the login page to access your account.
- If youâ€™re a new user, click on "sign up" to create an account. Fill in the required fields and submit.
- Once registered, you can use your email and password to log in and access all features.

2. Navigation

Using the navbar and footer

- The navbar at the top of each page provides quick access to various sections, including grades, subjects, user profile, and the User Guide.
- The footer provides additional links to resources, contact information, and support options.

3. Grade levels

- Select your grade level (from Grade 1 to Grade 12) to access customized content relevant to your curriculum.
- Each grade has subject-specific resources, including quizzes, videos, and assignments.

4. Subjects and content

- After selecting a grade, choose a subject (e.g., Math, English, Science) to access detailed content.
- Each subject offers structured learning materials, including text-based lessons, interactive quizzes, and tutorial videos.

5. Quizzes

- Each subject includes quizzes to help reinforce learning.
- Select a quiz, answer the questions, and submit to view your score and receive feedback on each answer.

6. Tutorial Videos

- Video tutorials are available for various topics, providing visual and auditory learning support.
- Access the videos directly within the subject pages, and play or pause them as needed.

7. AI audio assistance

- Use the AI audio assistance to listen to explanations for each topic. This feature can be accessed in the subject content sections.
- Click the "listen" button to start the audio, and use playback controls to pause or repeat any section.

8. Essay assistance

- The essay assistance tool helps improve your writing skills.
- Enter your essay topic, follow prompts to create your essay, and receive AI-generated suggestions for improvement.

9. Scan to extract text

- This feature allows you to scan printed materials to extract text for further study or analysis.
- Use the camera or upload an image of the text, and the system will convert it to digital text, ready for copying or further assistance.

10. Additional features

- User profile: Manage your personal information and track your progress.
- Help and support: Access this guide anytime, and reach out to support if you have questions.

We hope this guide helps you maximize your learning experience with the AI tutor system!
